Call Of The Children

Explore Issues Find Representative Send Message

This website exists because my daughter cares a lot. She was frustrated with watching bad things happen in the world and feeling powerless to do anything about them. Eventually this will become a resource to help empower kids to reach out to their elected representatives and make their voices heard.

This is clearly a very early proof of concept and work in progress but if you would like to contribute or have any suggestions please visit the Github repo and submit a pull request or contact me via Twitter.
